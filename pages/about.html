Team Phoenix is a FIRST Robotics team based in Nashua, New Hampshire. Our core philosophy centers around our student based leadership structure, helping every student develop skills imperative to future success. Students learn to take initiative, to communicate professionally, and to advocate for themselves and others; and in doing so, they foster a collaborative and supportive environemnt in which all members feel safe and comfortable. By upholding these values, we guaruntee the fullfillment of our mission statement: Changing the Lives of Youth Through Robotics.

Giving Back to the Community

Outreach

Advocacy

We launched "Reignite STEM" by submitting House Resolution 9 to advocate for equal STEM education in New Hampshire high schools. Our team continues to develop this initiative in preparation for a formal House Bill.

EarthFIRST

Our goal at EarthFIRST is to integrate sustainability into every aspect of the FIRST experience. We urge groups to reduce waste, reuse and recycle, and come up with innovative ways to make robotics more ecologically friendly. We are strengthening robotics sustainability through cooperation, education, and mutual dedication.

FIRST LEGO League

Team Phoenix hosts annual FLL open houses and summer camps to start 5 FLL teams in 2024 and 9 FLL teams in 2025. Both years we’ve had an all girls team and this year we’ve expanded to four homeschool teams, including one in Ohio! We also distribute FLL flyers to children during their annual Trunk or Treat event to encourage program participation.

International STEM

We established an FLL-based robotics curriculum in Manila, Philippines, by donating a SPIKE kit to a local school and working with school officials to reignite their robotics curriculum. We are also working with two schools in India to implement a robotics curriculum in the school and donated another SPIKE kit. In addition we visited a rural school for tribal children to introduce them to STEM and FIRST.

Robot Demos

Team Phoenix supports the Merrimack NH Drive-through Food Pantry with multiple annual food drives. We have collected and distributed 78 full Thanksgiving meal baskets over the last three years.

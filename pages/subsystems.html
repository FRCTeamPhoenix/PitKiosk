Robot Design

Subsystems

SYS-01

Drive

The drivetrain is the robot's mobility system. Our robot utilizes a swerve drive system, offering exceptional agility and precision by allowing independent control of each wheel's speed and direction. We use MK4i swerve modules 4” MK4i Billet Wheels and L2 gearing. The bot has a free speed of 15.5 ft./second.

SYS-02

Shooter

Our shooter is custom designed and has a fixed hood, powered by a Kraken x60, with a kicker powered by a Kraken x44. It utilizes 2 SDS 4 in. Brass Flywheel (1.5 lbs each) and has a 1:1 gear ratio via belts. Additionally, we use the WestCoast Products GreyT 7" ID Turret, with a Kraken x60, allowing for a 360 degree range of motion.

SYS-04

Intake

Our intake has a "slapdown" design, and acts as retaining wall for the back of the hopper when deployed. The reduction on the pivot is 1:20. Our intake features 4 live rollers and 1 dead roller, with 2 of them having 2'' compliant wheels and the other 2 rollers being polycarbonate tube with a silicon sock for grip. The frontmost roller creates a 0.25'' of compression of the fuel

SYS-05

Indexer

Our indexer consists of horizontal rollers and one vertical axle with wheels near our kicker, fondly nicknamed "the blender". Rollers are wrapped with Cats Tongue tape. The indexer contains 3 full width rollers (with 3D printed parts to assist with centering) and 3 shorter rollers to fit between the MK4i swerve modules.

SYS-03

Photon Vision

Photon Vision leverages cameras and image processing to determine target distance, angle, and position. The system primarily tracks AprilTags on the field, enabling precise autonomous alignment and scoring. We use an orange pi.
